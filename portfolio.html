Why OHIO ABC?

At the very beginning of 2016, I was looking for a Chinese roommate. It was no way for me to give out my message except for posting on Facebook. Then I bought domain name, rent host and built OHIO ABC. I am doing technical work as well as marketing work. Until now, there are more than 4,000 registered users as well as 10,000 posts on this website, which made it the biggest Ohio Chinese community.
